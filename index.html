About DiNaDrawing

"Drawing na naman" — this is what we often say when plans with friends or family never really happen.

We get excited with "outing tayo!" or "tara, dagat this Friday!" but when the day comes, there's no final place, no time, and sometimes no one shows up. A fun idea ends up as just words in the group chat.

This "drawing culture" is something we laugh about, but it can also be disappointing. Canceled plans mean lost time with friends, wasted weekends, and fewer memories together. Most of the time, it's not because people don't want to meet — it's because planning is messy. Too many chats, too many "sino ang pwede?" questions, and no clear decision about who will do what or how much everyone should contribute.

That's why we created DiNaDrawing. We know how much Filipinos value being together — barkada nights, family reunions, school projects, or even simple food trips. The problem isn't the desire to bond, it's the lack of an easy way to plan.

DiNaDrawing is here to make that easier. It helps groups vote on dates and venues, assign tasks, split contributions, and remind everyone of their roles. All details stay in one place, so nothing gets lost in the chat. Most importantly, it turns plans into reality.

Our dream is simple: to help every Filipino barkada, family, and group proudly say, "Di na drawing, tuloy na."

FAQs/
Support

Do I need to download an app? +

Nope! DiNaDrawing works straight from your browser. Just click the link or enter the code — no installs, no hassle.

How do I join an event? +

You can join by entering the event code or clicking the invitation link from the host.

What if I forget my task or contribution? +

Don't worry — DiNaDrawing saves your progress automatically and sends reminders.

Can I use this on my phone? +

Yes! It's mobile-friendly and works on any modern browser.

Is this free? +

Yes, DiNaDrawing is free to use for all users.
