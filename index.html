About DiNaDrawing

"Drawing na naman" — this is what we often say when plans with friends never happen.

Most of the time, it's not because people don't want to meet — it's because planning is messy.

That's why we created DiNaDrawing. We know how much Filipinos value being together.

It helps groups vote on dates and venues, assign tasks, split contributions, and remind everyone of their roles.

Our dream is simple: to help every Filipino barkada, family, and group proudly say, "Di na drawing, tuloy na."
